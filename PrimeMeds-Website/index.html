Sildenafil (Generic Viagra)

Sildenafil is used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED). It works by increasing blood flow to the penis during sexual stimulation. This helps you achieve and maintain an erection suitable for sexual activity.

Key Benefits:

  • Effective within 30-60 minutes
  • Lasts for 4-6 hours
  • Proven effectiveness in clinical trials
  • Most prescribed ED treatment worldwide

Dosage:

Available in 25mg, 50mg, and 100mg tablets. Your healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dose for you.

Tadalafil (Generic Cialis)

Tadalafil is used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED). It works by relaxing blood vessels and increasing blood flow to the penis during sexual stimulation.

Key Benefits:

  • Effective within 30-45 minutes
  • Lasts up to 36 hours ("weekend pill")
  • Can be taken with or without food
  • Lower dose daily option available

Dosage:

Available in 2.5mg, 5mg, 10mg, and 20mg tablets. Your healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dose for you.

Vardenafil (Generic Levitra)

Vardenafil is used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED). It works by increasing blood flow to the penis to help men get and keep an erection.

Key Benefits:

  • Effective within 25-60 minutes
  • Lasts for 4-5 hours
  • Can be effective for men with diabetes
  • Less affected by high-fat meals than sildenafil

Dosage:

Available in 5mg, 10mg, and 20mg tablets. Your healthcare provider will determine the appropriate dose for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are generic medications as effective as brand name?
Yes, generic medications contain the same active ingredients as their brand-name counterparts and are required by the FDA to have the same quality, strength, purity, and stability. The main differences are in the inactive ingredients and appearance, which do not affect the medication's performance.
Is this medication FDA-approved?
Yes, all our medications are FDA-approved and dispensed by licensed US pharmacists.
How does the online consultation work?
You'll complete a brief health questionnaire that will be reviewed by one of our licensed physicians. If approved, your prescription will be issued and filled. If additional information is needed, our medical team may contact you for clarification.
Is my information kept private?
Absolutely. We are HIPAA compliant and use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and health information. All packages are shipped in discreet packaging with no indication of the contents.
How soon will I receive my medication?
Most orders are processed within 24 hours and shipped via priority mail. You should receive your medication within 2-3 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're unsatisfied for any reason, contact our customer service team for a full refund. Please note that due to safety regulations, we cannot accept returns of prescription medications.
Do you accept insurance?
We do not directly bill insurance companies. However, we provide documentation that you can submit to your insurance for possible reimbursement. Our prices are often lower than insurance copays for these medications.
